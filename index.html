Convert Small Letters to Capital in Excel

If you work with data in Excel, converting lowercase to uppercase can be necessary for consistency. To convert small letter to capital in Excel, simply use the UPPER() function:

  1. Type =UPPER(A1) where A1 is the cell containing the text you want to convert.
  2. Drag the formula down to apply it to other cells.
  3. Your text will instantly be converted to uppercase.

Switch Uppercase to Lowercase in Word

Converting uppercase to lowercase in Word is simple. Use Word’s “Change Case” function for quick adjustments:

  1. Highlight the text you wish to convert.
  2. Go to the Home tab and select the Change Case button in the Font section.
  3. Choose lowercase from the dropdown menu.

Alternatively, use the Shift + F3 shortcut to toggle between uppercase, lowercase, and title case.
